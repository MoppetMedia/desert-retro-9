Portfolio

Colors of the Desert

Simple acrylic paintings of desert landscapes. These are love letters to the golden hour. I use a muted, vintage-inspired palette, where each piece captures the soul of wild stillness. My color palette leans toward warm, earthy tones—ochres, siennas, and muted greens—reflecting the natural hues of the desert.

Desert Sketches

Hand-drawn travel illustrations of people of the desert. These are quiet studies of life shaped by sun, sand, and resilience. I choose graphite pencils that offer both precision and softness. Brands like Faber-Castell 9000 and Staedtler Mars Lumograph are among my favourite.

Drift Travel-zine

As both photographer and illustrator for indie travel publications, I bring a storyteller's touch to every page. I have a love for off-the-map places and quiet moments. I document desert highways, flea markets, canyon trails, and roadside diners with nostalgic charm.
